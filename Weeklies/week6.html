Weekly 6 - Xenofeminism

By Kristin Wong

What is the purpose of XENOFEMINISM?

Xenofeminism, abbreviated as XF, constructs a feminism adapted to our complex reality. It exists to address and suit a future in which the realization of gender jusstice and feminist emanicipation contribute and apply to the entire universe. XF runs off of the believe that "natural" does not exist, and that the concept itself is ridiculous and is the reason for alienation. XF is also a rationalism in the way that if someone were to argue it was radical, you could point out that patriarchy is radical in the same exact way. The goal of XF is that there is no "feminine" or "masculine", but rather the goal that everyone speaks as no one in particular, thereby putting everyone on a level playing field. Xenofeminism exists to address gender inequality in way where gender and other limiting aspect of our culture no longer exist, making us also equal.
