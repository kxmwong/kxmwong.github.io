Biography!

Kristin is a multi-media artist working in photography, video, and graphic design. She is currently a sophomore studying at USC School of Cinematic Arts as a Media Arts and Practice major.

When she’s not working on her computer or making new art, she’s pursuing her passion of working with kids through Troy Camp as Director of Camp Programming.

One day, when she gets really good at technology, Kristin would love to work as a Storyboard Artist for Pixar Studios!

Other passions and interests include hiking, journaling, drawing, mental health advocacy, reproductive rights, painting her own jeans, the city of Portland in Oregon, and collecting hydroflasks.
